## Our Project:
The project we will be working on this semester involves designing a website for vendors, importers and traders to market their trucks in Egypt. We will be designing a database to be able to classify the types of trucks and their features, as well as storing information about the vendors.
Vendors will be able to add, edit and view their ads and the users can "bookmark" ads for later viewing.
This type of online market will be the first of its kind in Egypt and we are proud to be working on it.
